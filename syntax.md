## üìú 11. Grammar Rules (Synergex Syntax Laws)

1. **All expressions must begin and end with a system or kernel.**
2. **Feedback loops must be closed**: `‚àÆ( ... ‚Üí ... ‚Üí ... )`
3. **Emergence (`‚äó`) requires irreducibility.**
4. **Isomorphism (`‚â£`) and meta-isomorphism (`‚âã`) must be justified by kernels or structure.**
5. **Recursive structures must be bounded.**
6. **Open boundaries (`‚àû`) require at least one external interaction.**
7. **Probabilistic operators (`‚Ñô`, `‚àø`) must specify context.**
8. **Abstraction layers (`Œõ`) must be hierarchically consistent.**
